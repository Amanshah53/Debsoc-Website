The overview of our upcoming fest; Debathon to be held on 9th and 10th of March 2024:

3.Fast Facts Frenzy (KBC Theme) : Dive into the world of KBC with Fastest finger first.

6.Cultural Eve. : Let the beat of the drums and the sway of the dancers transport you to a world of celebration and unity at our Cultural Eve!

Schedule

Beyond the Bookshelf

Embark on a captivating journey through the realm of literature with the esteemed journalist and author, Mrs. Geeta Shree at our book discussion event, "Beyond the Bookshelf". Unveil the layers of narratives, celebrate the art of storytelling and immerse yourself in a riveting conversation, where each page unfolds a universe of ideas waiting to be explored. Join us in celebrating the magic that lies within the pages.

10:30 AM - 12:00 PM K.P. Nautiyal Auditorium, GEHU

Charचा - The Group Discussion

Explore the art of expression in our upcoming group discussion event, where participants converge to delve into insightful conversations, exchange diverse viewpoints and refine their communication prowess. Join us for a dynamic and inclusive forum, fostering collaborative thinking and meaningful dialogue – a unique opportunity to enhance your verbal articulation and engage in thought-provoking discussions.

01:30 PM - 04:00 PM K.P. Nautiyal Auditorium, GEHU

Fast Facts Frenzy (KBC Theme - Carsh Prize Rs.5000)

Experience your adrenaline boost through the roof as our anchors present rapid-fire questions. Only the quickest and most astute participants will emerge victorious.

04:30 PM - 05:30 PM K.P. Nautiyal Auditorium, GEHU

परिचर्चा - The Panel Discussion

"पहचान पर पुनर्विचार व विकास की पुनर्परिभाषा उत्तराखंड के भू - कानून पर एक परीचर्चा"
Dive into riveting discussions as expert panelists share insights. Your chance to steer the conversation with questions. Join the intellectual exchange!.
Re-thinking identities and Re-defining growth and development. A Debate on land laws in Uttarakhand.
- Mr. Indu Kumar Pandey, Former Chief Secretary of Uttarakhand and esteemed ex-IAS officer.
- Mr. B P Nautiyal, Senior Advocate of Uttarakhand High Court.
- Mr. Jay Singh Rawat, a seasoned journalist with over 40 years of experience.

9:30 AM - 11:00 AM K.P. Nautiyal Auditorium, GEHU

Just A Minute

Join us for an exhilarating 'Just a Minute' session presented by Debating Society of Graphic Era University.Test your impromptu speaking skills in this fast-paced, adrenaline-fueled challenge. Dare to speak your mind in 60 seconds or less, and unleash your inner orator!.

11:00 AM - 02:00 PM K.P. Nautiyal Auditorium, GEHU

Cultural Eve.

Join us for an enchanting evening featuring captivating & energetic performances. Celebrate the beauty of global diversity and unity in a joyous atmosphere. Embrace the richness of cultures at this unforgettable event!

05:00 PM - 05:30 PM K.P. Nautiyal Auditorium, GEHU
